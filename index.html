Top three activities to do in Eskisehir

Explore Odunpazarı

Stroll through the historic district filled with colorful houses, artisan shops, and lively cafés.

Stroll along the Porsuk River

Enjoy a relaxing walk or a scenic boat ride along the river that runs through the city center.

Visit Sazova Park

Discover a mix of science, art, and culture at this expansive park with unique attractions.
